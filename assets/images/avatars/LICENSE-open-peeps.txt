Open Peeps avatar assets

The SVG files in this directory are deterministic variants generated from
the DiceBear Open Peeps avatar style using seeds VitalySync-01 through
VitalySync-24.

Open Peeps was created by Pablo Stanley and is available under the
Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Universal public-domain dedication.
