Avataaars professional avatar assets

The SVG files prefixed with avataaars_ are deterministic variants generated
from the DiceBear Avataaars style. They use curated clothing, hairstyle,
expression, accessory, and color options for the student, working
professional, young professional, and freelancer collections.

Avataaars was created by Pablo Stanley and is available for personal and
commercial use. These files are DiceBear-generated remixes of that work.
