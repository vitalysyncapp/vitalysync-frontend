Personas avatar assets

The SVG files in this directory are deterministic variants generated from
the DiceBear Personas avatar style using seeds VitalySync-01 through
VitalySync-24.

"Personas by Draftbit" was created by Draftbit and is licensed under the
Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International license (CC BY 4.0). These
files are DiceBear-generated remixes of that work.
